Who are we?


GB Create are a team of Chartered Engineers in Liverpool with over 25 years of experience, led by Dr. Gregor Beattie. We cover Liverpool and the whole of Merseyside. Whether it's structural surveys or structural engineering design, our clients are at the centre of everything we do. From residential properties to commercial developments, Greg and the team have helped out a vast range of clients over the years.


Greg started his structural engineering career with a large international company, before founding GB Create over 15 years ago. Using his expertise as a structural engineer in Liverpool, and former construction worker, Greg and GB Create now deliver projects of all shapes and sizes. Whilst structural surveys and engineering design have always been a key part of the business, GB Create have also completed numerous construction projects over the years, small to large. Now focusing primarily on structural surveys and engineering design work, whether you need a structural crack looking at, or to take advantage of our full structural surveying or design services, Greg and the team are here to help.
