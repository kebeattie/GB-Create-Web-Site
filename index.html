Who are we?


GB Create are a team of Chartered Engineers (CEng MICE) with over 20 years of experience, led by Dr. Greg Beattie. Whether it's structural surveys or engineering design, our clients are at the centre of everything we do. From residential properties to derelict commercial developments, Greg and the team have seen it all helping out a vast range of clients over the years. Rest assured, with GB Create, your structural survey and engineering needs are in safe hands.


Greg founded GB Create over 10 years ago, using his structural engineering and construction expertise to take on jobs of all shapes and sizes. Whilst structural surveys and engineering design have always been a key part of the business, GB Create has also completed numerous construction projects over the years, small to large. Now focusing solely on structural surveys and engineering design work, whether you need a structural crack looking at, or a potential new home surveyed, Greg and the team are here to help.
